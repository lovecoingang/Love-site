Economics

The Love token is designed for longevity — not a flash launch, but a purposeful path toward economic empowerment and liquidity growth. Starting at a lean 15% liquidity, this project flips traditional crypto norms by intentionally growing liquidity through community-oriented donations over time.
We're currently expanding liquidity toward a 30% target through milestone-based releases and incentive-driven growth. Then will gradually release a percentage of the reward pool annually to keep rewards coming while protecting the long term value of LOVE.

Low Liquidity Advantage

Launching with only 0.025 SOL in the pool wasn’t just a technical choice — it was a strategic move to protect early adopters and prevent front-running bots from draining the pool. While trades are currently micro-sized (under $1), this gives investors the opportunity to accumulate early without major volatility.
Currently airdrops are manual and will be given within 24 hours for transparency and trust. As the liquidity pool deepens and automation improves, so will the scale and simplicity of transactions.
